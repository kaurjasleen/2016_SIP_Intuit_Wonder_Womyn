THE GIRL WHO DIDN'T GIVE UP ON LEARNING









Patience was forced to drop out of school when her parents refused to pay the fees for her education. But the idea that this was the end of her education never crossed her mind. Nothing was going to stop her from learning. From cultivating crops to selling solar lamps, Patience took on jobs to pay for her own school fees. However, she hit another obstacle when her mother fell ill. Patience had to nurse her mother and look after her siblings, which meant her studies suffered.
